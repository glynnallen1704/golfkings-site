Support

If in doubt — hello@golfkings.co.za

GolfKings is built by one person. Email hello@golfkings.co.za with anything — accounts, pools, picks, bugs, feature requests. Replies usually within a day, faster on weekends because that's when the golf is on.

How to delete your account

Email hello@golfkings.co.za from the address you signed up with. We remove your profile, picks, pool memberships, push tokens, and analytics identifiers within 30 days. No questions asked.

The app crashed

Sorry. Two things help:

  1. Reopen — we ship over-the-air bug fixes regularly, and a fresh launch picks them up.
  2. Email hello@golfkings.co.za with what you were doing when it crashed and your iPhone/Android model. Crash reports come through Expo so if you sent one, it probably already landed.

Common questions

Why can't I pick four world-#1s?

Every team has a combined world-rank target of ≥ 100. Four #1s would be a combined rank of 4 — well under the target. The constraint forces real choices instead of "everyone picks Scheffler."

One of my golfers withdrew. What happens?

Your team continues with three live golfers + a substitution. For round-three or round-four substitutions, we use the worst made-cut score of the day in their slot. R1/R2 withdrawals leave the slot empty and you take the points hit — pick again next week.

How does scoring work?

R1 + R2 sum all four players. R3 and R4 use the best three of four — the worst score that day is dropped. Lowest combined four-round total wins the week.

Where are the in-app purchases?

There aren't any. No subscriptions, no premium tier, no ads, no microtransactions. The app is free because we're not trying to monetise it — we just wanted to stop running our Sunday pool on a spreadsheet.

Why is my golfer's rank weird?

World ranks update Mondays at 07:00 UTC, sourced from OWGR. Lock-time ranks (what you see on a locked team) are frozen — your team shows the world ranks at the moment you locked picks, not whatever they have drifted to since.

Can I host a pool with people on Android and iOS?

Yes. The app is the same on both platforms; data syncs through InstantDB the moment a member joins.
