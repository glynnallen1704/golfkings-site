01 / 04 — How it works

Four golfers. One pool. Every Thursday.

  1. 01

    Run a pool

    Create a private pool, share the join code with your group chat — or join a public one and play against strangers if you must.

  2. 02

    Pick four

    Each PGA Tour week, pick four golfers from the field. A world-rank budget keeps it fair — no four-Schefflers teams.

  3. 03

    Lock the team

    Before Thursday's first tee shot, lock it in. After that it's set — your team is what you brought to the dance.

  4. 04

    Follow live

    Real leaderboards. Real cuts. Real auto-substitution when someone withdraws. Lowest combined score Sunday night wins the week.

03 / 04 — Features

Everything a pool needs. Nothing it doesn't.

Live leaderboards

Round-by-round scoring direct from the tour. Cuts, withdrawals, and final positions update through the weekend.

Season-long standings

Your Order of Merit across every PGA Tour event. Find out who's actually the best at this — and who just got lucky once.

Auto-substitution

Pick gets injured? Misses the cut? The app substitutes the worst made-cut score of the day so your weekend isn't ruined.

Private & public pools

Invite-only by default. Share a join code with the group chat. Or jump into a public pool and play against strangers.

Notifications that matter

Team locked. Withdrawal alert. Top-three climb. Final results. Every notification is opt-in, opt-out — your call.

News, kept short

An editorial News tab with tournament results, world ranking shifts, and major previews. Written like a column, not a press release.
