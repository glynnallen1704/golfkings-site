Privacy

Last updated · 2026-05-26

This is the privacy policy for the GolfKings iOS and Android app (bundle ID com.glynnallen1704.golfkings), operated by Glynn Allen. Contact: hello@golfkings.app.

Plain-English version: we collect the minimum we need to run a fantasy golf app, we don't sell or share your data for advertising, and you can delete your account at any time by emailing the address above.

What we collect

Data Why Linked to you?
Email address Sign-in via magic code; account recovery Yes
Display name and handle Shown in pools and on leaderboards Yes
Avatar photo (optional, you upload it) Shown next to your name Yes
Apple/Google push token Sending push notifications you opt into Yes
Anonymous device identifier App lifecycle analytics so we know which builds users are on Yes
Your picks, team history, pool memberships Core app functionality Yes
Anonymous product-interaction events Improving the app (which screens, which features, retention) Yes
Crash and performance diagnostics Bug fixing No

We do not collect: location, contacts, health, financial information, payment information, browsing history outside the app, advertising identifiers, or anything used for cross-app tracking.

Third-party services we use

Service What it does Data it sees
InstantDB Database + sync for accounts, pools, picks, scores Everything in the "Linked to you" rows above
Sanity Content management for in-app news articles None of your data — articles are read from your device
Slash Golf (RapidAPI) Tournament schedules, fields, live scores, world rankings None of your data — server-side only
PostHog (US-region cloud) Product analytics — which screens are used, which actions are taken A pseudonymous user ID, your email, and interaction events. No payment info, no contacts, no location.
Expo / EAS Build infrastructure and push notification delivery Push tokens for notification routing
Unsplash Cover images on news articles None of your data

We do not use advertising networks, marketing trackers, or sell data to third parties.

Push notifications

You can toggle each push category on/off independently from Profile → Notifications inside the app. Default is on for scoring-related alerts (team live, withdrawals, top-three climbs, final results) and off for editorial content (articles, OWGR shifts, field announcements).

How long we keep your data

For as long as you have an account. If you ask us to delete your account (email hello@golfkings.app), we remove your profile, picks, pool memberships, push tokens, and analytics identifiers within 30 days. Aggregated and anonymised analytics may persist beyond that.

Children

GolfKings is rated 4+ but is not directed at children under 13. We don't knowingly collect data from anyone under 13. If you believe a child has signed up, email us and we'll delete the account.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy when the app changes. Material changes will be flagged in the app or via email to your registered address. The "Last updated" date at the top tracks the most recent revision.

Your rights

  • Access the data we have on you — email hello@golfkings.app.
  • Delete your account and associated data — same inbox.
  • Opt out of push notifications at any time — Profile → Notifications inside the app.
  • Disable analytics by signing out — we don't analytics-track anonymous (signed-out) users beyond crash diagnostics.

GolfKings has no real-money wagering, in-app purchases, or subscriptions. We don't take payment information.

Contact

Anything else, hello@golfkings.app.
